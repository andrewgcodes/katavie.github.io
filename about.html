About

Katherine Huang is the founder of Science & Us, an event that teaches high schoolers how to communicate complex ideas in exciting, clear ways through multimedia. She is also the co-director of MAHacks, an entrepreneurship-focused high school hackathon in Boston, and has completed research internships at Harvard University and UMass Lowell. Her work in computational biology and biotechnology has been recognized by the National Junior Science and Humanities Symposium, Massachusetts State Science and Engineering Fair, and the Journal of Emerging Investigators. She is a member of FIRST Robotics Competition Team 5962, recipient of the National Center for Women and Information Technology Aspirations in Computing Award, and president of several clubs at Lowell High School in Lowell, MA.
